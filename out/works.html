Predicting Salary in Latin America

This project uses machine learning models to predict salary across 11 Latin American countries. Using economic and demographic factors such as labor force participation, fertility rates, and more, we developed predictive models using Linear Regression, FFNN, Random Forests, and K-Nearest Neighbors. The results predict salary and provide insights for policymakers and organizations.

Statistical Insights into What Drives Article Shares

This project analyzes what factors make an online news article popular. We applied statistical inference and linear regression models (with log-transformed target) to investigate how structural features (title length, media count…), timing (weekday vs. weekend), sentiment polarity, and content category influence article shares on social media.
